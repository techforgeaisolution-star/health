What is DiseasePredict?

DiseasePredict is an AI-powered health screening system that uses advanced machine learning models to assess the risk of three major chronic diseases: Diabetes, Heart Disease, and Parkinson's Disease.

Our system analyzes various health indicators and biomarkers to provide risk assessments, helping users understand their potential health risks before visiting a healthcare provider.

97% Accuracy

Trained on verified clinical datasets

How It Works

Our prediction system uses three different machine learning models, each trained on disease-specific datasets:

  • Diabetes Model — Analyzes metabolic markers including glucose levels, BMI, insulin, age, blood pressure, and family history to assess diabetes risk.
  • Heart Disease Model — Examines cardiovascular indicators such as cholesterol, chest pain patterns, ECG results, maximum heart rate, and exercise tolerance.
  • Parkinson's Model — Uses voice biomarker analysis (jitter, shimmer, HNR, PPE) to detect early signs of Parkinson's disease from speech patterns.
